Methodology
How TrialMatch AI works
A three-stage pipeline from patient phenotype to ranked, structured trial matches.

Phenotype normalization

Patient data — diagnosis, biomarkers, prior therapies, performance status — is normalized into structured FHIR R4 resources and mapped to SNOMED CT concept codes for interoperable representation.

Eligibility extraction

AI extracts and parses free-text inclusion/exclusion criteria from 200K+ ClinicalTrials.gov studies, tagging each criterion with MeSH terms and clinical logic.

Alignment & ranking

Each trial receives a 0–100 match score based on criterion-level alignment between patient phenotype and trial eligibility. Criteria are individually flagged as met, partial, or unmet.
